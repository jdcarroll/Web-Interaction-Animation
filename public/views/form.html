Step 3 (How Does Rebel Work?)

How does it Work?

The way that Rebel works is very simple. It will monitor the incoming and out going traffic trough your router and report back details about your network statics in a end user friendly way by:

  1. Monitoring Network traffic going in and out of your router.
  2. tying that network traffic to your registered devices.
  3. Finally Calculating that total network usage and cross referencing it with your provided network speeds.
